About Me

I am a devoted follower of God and attribute all my achievements and blessings to Jesus Christ. I believe in giving back and being a channel of positive impact—striving always to leave others better than I found them.

My values were shaped by my parents, the Late Mr. Anthony Aliu Jubrilla and Mrs. Olawunmi Esther Jubrilla. From my father, I learned credibility, hard work, and integrity; from my mother, empathy and honesty. I bring 10+ years across banking and operations with a focus on compliance, AML/KYC and risk management.

I run a mentorship program for young professionals anchored on equality, inclusivity and respect. Awards include: Most Influential Compliance Professional (2023), Best KYC Supervisor of the Year (Polaris Bank, 2022), and Excellence in Mentorship (2021).
