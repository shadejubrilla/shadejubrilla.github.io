PEP detection and escalation

A focused review that protected the bank from a sensitive risk and led to a stronger escalation framework.

1High risk case prevented
NewEscalation template created
TrainedAnalysts across the unit

Context

During a review an unusual pattern surfaced in the profile of a prospective customer. Links to public office and sensitive networks were not fully documented, and there was a risk that the case could move forward without adequate scrutiny.

Approach

  • Rechecked external and internal sources for any political connections.
  • Mapped related entities, accounts, and owners to build a clearer picture.
  • Prepared a detailed memo that presented the risk in clear language for senior review.
  • Worked with compliance and legal partners to confirm next steps and documentation.
  • Turned the experience into an escalation template for future high risk profiles.

Outcome

  • The institution avoided onboarding a customer that presented a serious risk.
  • The new template and process improved consistency across future reviews.
  • Analysts learned how to recognise subtle patterns that point to political exposure.
