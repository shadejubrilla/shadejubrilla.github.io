Continuity under crisis

Designing a simple playbook that kept KYC work moving during disruption and reduced incomplete submissions.

30%Fewer incomplete submissions
ZeroRegulatory misses in the period
OneClear playbook for future events

Context

A system issue disrupted normal KYC routing. Branches and units started sending requests through unstructured channels. The risk of lost files, delays, and incomplete submissions increased by the hour.

Approach

  • Introduced a central SharePoint intake with defined fields for each request.
  • Set minimum requirements that had to be met before a case could move forward.
  • Created a short guide and shared it quickly with teams so they knew exactly what to do.
  • Documented the steps as a simple disaster recovery playbook for future incidents.

Outcome

  • Incomplete submissions dropped by more than thirty percent.
  • KYC work continued with control and traceability even while systems were unstable.
  • The organisation gained a reusable model for disruption readiness.
