Full biography

Afolashade Joy Jubrilla is a strategist, author, and human development advocate redefining what it means to lead, adapt, and stay relevant in an era of automation and accelerated change.


With over a decade of multidisciplinary experience spanning banking, operations, risk, portfolio management, business development, and international trade, she brings a unique perspective on how systems, strategy, and people intersect to create sustainable progress.


Her insights bridge the gap between governance, innovation, and human adaptability, helping organisations and professionals simplify complexity, build clarity, and strengthen decision-making in volatile environments.


She is the author of Surviving the AI Recession: How to Stay Relevant, Resilient, and Ready in an Automated World — an Amazon Top 3 Bestseller in Business Leadership and Career Guides. The book has sparked international dialogue on the future of work, leadership resilience, and the power of human judgment in a machine-driven age.


Beyond her professional expertise, Afolashade is a passionate advocate for girls’ education, continuous learning, and body positivity. She believes every individual — regardless of background, orientation, or appearance — deserves the freedom to learn, grow, and live without judgment.


Her mission is to inspire professionals and organisations to adapt with purpose, lead with empathy, and build relevance in a world that refuses to stand still.
