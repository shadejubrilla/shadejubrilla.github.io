About Afolashade

Afolashade Joy Jubrilla is a global thought leader, strategist, and author of Surviving the AI Recession: How to Stay Resilient, Relevant, and Ready in an Automated World. Known for her sharp insights on adaptability, human resilience, and systems transformation, she helps professionals and organisations redesign relevance in an age defined by automation. Her work blends governance, process strategy, and empathy, guiding leaders to stay human in a world that’s becoming more mechanical by the minute.


Extended biography

Afolashade Joy Jubrilla is a Nigerian-born author, strategist, and global voice on adaptability and human relevance in the digital age. She is the author of Surviving the AI Recession, a groundbreaking work exploring how professionals and organisations can stay future-ready amidst automation, disruption, and shifting global economies.


With a background spanning operations, credit, risk, compliance, business development, governance, and development policy, Afolashade has worked across sectors, from banking to international trade and global mobility. Her experience managing complex systems, portfolios, and processes shaped her conviction that resilience is not an event but an ecosystem.


Her mission is clear: to build future-fit humans - individuals and institutions equipped to thrive through uncertainty. Her insights bridge the gap between governance, innovation, and human adaptability, helping organisations and professionals simplify complexity, build clarity, and strengthen decision-making in volatile environments.


She writes, speaks, and consults on adaptability, ethical leadership, and system redesign, helping professionals transition from overwhelmed to prepared


Her voice has been featured across major publications including The Guardian, and Nigerian Tribune , where she is recognised as one of the leading African thinkers shaping the conversation around AI and the future of work.
